Decoding Embedded Systems

Understanding the core components: Microprocessor, Microcontroller, and Embedded System.

Microprocessor

The "brain" of a computer. It's a CPU that requires external memory, I/O, and other components to function. High processing power.

Microcontroller

A compact computer-on-a-chip. It integrates a CPU, memory, and I/O peripherals onto a single integrated circuit. Self-contained.

Embedded System

A dedicated computer system designed to perform specific functions within a larger mechanical or electrical system. Uses microcontrollers or microprocessors.
